PIZZA DIP

MENU PIZZA

  • INGREDIENTS
  • STEPS
  • FINAL DISH

INGREDIENTS

  • 16.3 oz can Grands biscuit dough
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 tsp parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 3 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 cup marinara / pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 8 slices pepperoni (optional)

STEPS

  1. Cut biscuits in half, roll into balls, coat with butter + seasoning.
  2. Arrange dough balls around skillet.
  3. Spread cheese mixture in center.
  4. Add marinara sauce.
  5. Top with mozzarella + seasoning.
  6. Add pepperoni (optional).
  7. Bake 25–30 minutes at 350°F.
